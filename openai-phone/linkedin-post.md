A press rumor says OpenAI is building a phone. The job postings say more.

We pulled all 746 active OpenAI postings from the Skillenai job index. **19 of them sit on a team OpenAI calls "Consumer Devices."** The team description, copied verbatim from a posting: *"end-to-end hardware and software systems… custom silicon, embedded systems, operating systems."*

The receipts:
• Operating Systems Engineer — kernel, secure boot, **battery and thermal-aware tuning**
• Camera ISP Software Engineer (image-signal-processor silicon role)
• Embedded SWE for Consumer Devices
• Sensing Engineer on the "Neosensing" team
• An "SMS Prototype Handling Specialist" inside an OpenAI **Secure Manufacturing & Stealth** team whose stated job is keeping prototypes confidential
• A Strategic Finance lead for **COGS & Supply Chain** — you only hire that role for a physical product

The big surprise — the article's open question was *"will Jony Ive be forced to ship Android?"* Our data: **zero postings mention AOSP or Android Open Source.** The 10 Android engineers OpenAI is hiring are all on the ChatGPT app, not Consumer Devices. The kernel/embedded/Linux/secure-boot signature points to a **custom Linux-based OS, not an Android fork.**

Conspicuously *not* yet staffed in volume: acoustic, RF, antenna, mechanical engineers. Either deferred to an ODM partner, or the first device isn't a phone-with-its-own-modem at all (a Wi-Fi/companion device first is consistent with the data).
